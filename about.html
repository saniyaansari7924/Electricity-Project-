About Project

Peak Load Prediction helps identify periods of high electricity demand using historical household power consumption data.

Project Objective

To predict future electricity consumption and identify peak demand periods for better energy management.

Benefits

  • Efficient Energy Distribution
  • Reduced Power Overload
  • Improved Grid Stability
  • Smart Energy Planning

Why This Project?

Electricity demand is increasing every day. Identifying peak load periods helps reduce power failures, improves grid stability, and enables smarter energy management decisions.

Benefits

  • Efficient Energy Distribution
  • Reduced Overloading
  • Smart Grid Support
  • Better Resource Planning
